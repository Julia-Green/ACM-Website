UA Litte Rock ACM Student Chapter

ComputerDonationDrive

Saturday September 19th

9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

UA Little Rock Parking Lot 8

Learn More

The UA Little Rock ACM Student Chapter has identified some of the difficulties being faced by Students across the nation. Currently, students are having to adapt to a new, online learning environment due to the COVID-19 virus. We see that there is a need for access to computing devices for students taking classes online, and feel that this is an issue that we are well equipped to help our local community overcome.

From 9:00 A.M. to 5:00 P.M. on Saturday, September 19th, we are hosting charity donation drive. We ask that the community donate electronic devices that we can distribute to students who lack the necessary equipment to succeed in their education with this new online-centric modality. A table will be set up in parking lot 8 at UA Little Rock where we will accept equipment of any age, brand, and condition. We encourage people to donate items even if they believe they are of no value as we may use them for parts to combine with other equipment to make usable machines. We will also accept cash donations if that is the wish of the donor. We will hand out receipts for tax deduction purposes to those who desire proof of donation. All proceeds that come from this event will be donated to local students and any left over funds will be given to a local Pulaski County charity providing COVID-19 relief.

Equipment that cannot be repaired or used for parts will be recycled at an official electronics recycling location. In order to ensure safety of student workers and donors, workers will be provided with proper PPE including: face masks, face shields, cleaning wipes, and isopropyl alcohol. After collection, equipment will be disinfected and stored in the ACM Clubroom on campus. The equipment will then be assessed for functionality and nessicary repairs will be made. All storage devices will be wiped and have fresh operating systems installed so that users will have little to no setup required. We will make the devices available to UA Little Rock students at first; however, if the resources outpace the demand at UA Little Rock then we will reach out to other local universities and schools.

We hope to see you on September 19th!
